Artisan crafting notes (LiveKnowledge-style domain).

The guild tracks which items can be crafted from which materials.
Iron ore and coal are required to smelt iron bars.
Iron bars and leather are required to craft iron daggers.
Herbalists brew healing potions from moonflower and spring water.

Profit on an item depends on sell price minus material cost. Iron daggers sell for 45–60 gold; material cost is about 20 gold. Healing potions sell for 12–18 gold; herbs cost 3 gold.

Demand cycles: iron weapons sell better in winter (cycle winter). Potions sell better after raids (cycle raid_recovery).

We store facts as item/1, material/2, recipe/2 (item, required_material), profit/3 (item, low_gold, high_gold), demand_cycle/1.
